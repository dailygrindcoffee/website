Daily Grind Coffee

Our Story

For the past 18 years, Daily Grind Coffee has provided a cozy and welcoming atmosphere in the west end of Winnipeg. With friendly staff, soups made from scratch and home baked desserts, Daily Grind is a place for all ages to meet, study and connect. Live music, free Wi-Fi and comfortable seating encourage the building of relationships and community; here we strive to make you feel like you’re part of the Daily Grind family. With a large selection of fresh food and drinks, we offer a wide variety of options for dietary restrictions and modifications to make each visit a unique and flavourful one.
